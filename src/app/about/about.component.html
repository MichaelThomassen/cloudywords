About Cloudy Words

Made with ☁️ and ☕ by Michael Thomassen.

The game is designed for mobile devices in portrait mode, but it also works pretty well on desktop browsers too.

Some words may have multiple meanings, but only one or sometimes two definitions are shown to keep things simple.

All words in the game were originally generated by ChatGPT but have been reviewed and corrected by a human.

The source code is available on GitHub, where you can also report bugs and leave suggestions.

We use Google Analytics to track page visits and understand where our users come from (anonymously). No personal data is stored.

Have fun and enjoy the clouds! ☁️
