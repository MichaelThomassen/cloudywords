How to Play 🕹️

Guess the hidden word, one letter at a time!

  • 🌫️ You start with nothing but two clouds hiding the word.
  • ⌨️ Guess letters using the on-screen keyboard.
  • ✅ Correct letters appear in their positions between the clouds.
  • 🌤️ Find the first or last letter to remove that cloud and reveal the word’s full length.
  • ☁️ Your score starts at 10☁️ per word and decreases by 1☁️ for each incorrect guess (minimum 2☁️).

Game Structure: A Roguelite Word Experience 🎲

Cloudy Words is designed as a roguelite — a type of game where you start with almost nothing, and gradually unlock new tools and advantages through play.

At first, the game is deliberately hard 💀. But as you earn ☁️, you'll be able to unlock helpful upgrades that make guessing easier. You shape your own progression and playstyle 🎯.

If you later feel that the game becomes too easy, you can always refund individual upgrades to make the challenge feel right again 🔄.

Upgrades ⚙️

Once you've collected at least 25☁️, you’ll gain access to the upgrade menu. Upgrades can do things like:

  • 📂 Reveal the word’s category or definition
  • 🚫 Remove letters from the keyboard that are not part of the word
  • 🎁 Give you a free letter
  • 🔍 Show the word length earlier

For 50☁️, you can unlock the Daily Boost upgrade ⚡. This gives you the ability to boost 5 words each day, making them worth 10× the usual points 💥.

Daily Boost is perfect for speeding up progression or adding a little extra fun to your daily play session 🎉.

Word Rules 📖

All words in the game follow these rules:

  • 🔤 Words are in English and contain only basic letters A–Z (no symbols, spaces, or accents).
  • 📏 Each word is at least 5 letters long.
  • 🧠 Some words have multiple meanings, but usually only one definition is shown due to space limitations.

Learn While You Play 🧑‍🏫

Cloudy Words isn't just a game — it's also a great way to expand your English vocabulary 📚. Each word comes with a short definition, so you’ll not only guess, but also learn as you go.

We almost guarantee that you won’t know every single word in the game — and that’s the fun of it! Whether you're a language nerd or just curious, there’s always something new to discover 🕵️.
